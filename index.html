Area Guide
Estes Park & surrounding communities — what’s great, what to consider, how they compare

✅ Why This Market Works

🏔️Rocky Mountain National Park on your doorstep — 415 square miles of protected wilderness, year-round hiking, wildlife, and dramatic scenery
📈Strong long-term value retention — mountain-view properties within 40 minutes of Estes consistently appreciate faster than Larimer County averages
🏠Quality home stock in your price range — $750K–$1M buys genuinely high-finish homes with views and the architectural character you’re looking for
🌞300+ days of sunshine annually — cooler summers than Front Range cities, dramatically different lifestyle than Colorado Springs in the best way
🚗Loveland & Fort Collins within 45 minutes — full urban amenities, UCHealth Level II hospital, big box retail, CSU

⚠️ Things to Know

🔥Wildfire risk is real — verify specific parcel risk scores, defensible space, and insurance availability before committing
🛣️US-34 through Big Thompson Canyon is the primary access corridor — subject to closures after severe weather; know Hwy 36 via Lyons as your alternate
🏭Short-term rental restrictions vary by jurisdiction — verify Estes Park town, Larimer County, or HOA rules before purchase if income is a consideration
🕐Well water and septic are common outside town limits — inspection of both systems is critical; costs and permit requirements differ from city homes
